# Etimad AI Labs Private Limited — Business Objective

## Objective (summary)

To design, develop, fine-tune, and deploy artificial intelligence systems —
including AI agents, open-source language models, and agentic data pipelines —
that enable secure enterprise automation and digital transformation.

---

## What the Company does (technology)

Etimad AI Labs Private Limited is a technology company engaged in the research,
development, and deployment of artificial intelligence and machine learning
solutions for enterprises. The Company's work spans the full AI stack — from the
underlying data infrastructure, to the models, to the applications that put them
to use:

- **AI Agents** — Building autonomous and human-in-the-loop AI agents that
  execute multi-step business workflows across enterprise systems, with
  guardrails, observability, and audit trails.

- **Open-Source Model Fine-Tuning & Deployment** — Fine-tuning, optimising,
  quantising, and deploying open-source large language models inside the
  customer's own infrastructure (on-premise, private cloud, or air-gapped),
  ensuring data security and full customer ownership of the models.

- **Agentic Data Pipelines** — Engineering data ingestion, processing,
  embedding, and vector-database infrastructure that feed reliable agentic
  workflows at enterprise scale, with data lineage, quality, and governance.

- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) & Fine-Tuning** — Designing retrieval
  systems and applying model fine-tuning to deliver accurate, domain-specific AI
  performance, validated through evaluation-driven delivery.

- **Enterprise Applications** — Developing custom AI-native software
  applications and integrating AI capabilities into the existing systems,
  applications, and databases of customers.

- **Strategy & Consulting** — Providing advisory and consulting services in
  artificial intelligence strategy and digital transformation, including use-case
  assessment, solution architecture, implementation, and ongoing support.

The Company delivers these solutions with an emphasis on security, measurable
performance, and customer ownership of the resulting models, data, and systems.

---

## Main Objects (formal clause)

*The objects to be pursued by the Company are:*

1. To carry on the business of researching, designing, developing, building,
   training, fine-tuning, testing, deploying, hosting, and maintaining
   artificial intelligence and machine learning systems, including AI agents,
   agentic workflows, large language models (LLMs), and open-source foundation
   models, for enterprise automation and business process optimisation.

2. To fine-tune, customise, optimise, quantise, and deploy open-source and
   proprietary machine learning models within on-premise, private-cloud,
   air-gapped, or hybrid environments, enabling secure data handling and
   customer ownership of models and infrastructure.

3. To design, build, and operate data pipelines, data-ingestion and
   data-processing systems, embedding and vector-database infrastructure,
   retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems, and related data-engineering
   and data-governance solutions that support artificial intelligence and
   agentic workflows.

4. To develop, license, supply, and maintain computer software, applications,
   and platforms that embed artificial intelligence, and to integrate such
   products with the existing information systems of customers.

5. To provide consultancy, advisory, technical, training, and support services
   in artificial intelligence, machine learning, data engineering, and digital
   transformation to businesses, institutions, and government bodies in India
   and abroad.

6. To enable enterprises to adopt artificial intelligence and automation
   technologies through use-case assessment, solution architecture,
   implementation, deployment, and post-deployment support.
